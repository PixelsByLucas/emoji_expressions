    Rules 🌵

    Upload an image or paste an image link in the field above above and let's see how you're feeling today, we'll tell you with an emoji that really gets you. Images must be in .jpg format and the best one we can work with are pictures where your face isn't obstructed or obscured. Leave any buddies out of this analysis as we're just looking for one emotion from one face. Have fun and see how the your personality can be interpreted by emoji.

    About 🐱‍🐉

    This is a fun project created together by Becky Pollard and Lucas Knight exploring the use of API and the power of Microsoft's face recognition data, as part of HackerYou's Bootcamp program. Emoji are great, we had fun playing with them and hope you do too.
